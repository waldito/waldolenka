How to arrive?

It's easy :)

Pick your airport

Option 1: Prague Airport PRG

Prague Airport PRG > Train Station > Zábřeh na Moravě > Šumperk

Estimated travel duration: approx 4 hours

Take an AE Bus (Airport Express) to the Prague Main Train station and hop on a train that leaves you in Zábřeh na Moravě where you need to get off the train. Please confirm with the person who checks the tickets on the train. They will be able to estimate the time for you to get ready to leave the train.

Prague airport is directly connected to the main train station via a bus line. The Airport Express buses run from the airport to the train station every 30 minutes with an average travel time of 35 minutes. The bus operates from 5:30 am to 10:30 pm. A one-way ticket for the Airport Express bus costs around 3 Euros, one suitcase is included in the price. Tickets can be purchased from the driver, payment option is cash in Czech Koruna. It is better to take some Czech Korunas from an ATM at the airport before heading towards the city. Find more info


Check the AE bus connections and train schedule + book online. Most of the options are bookable online and let you select your preferred class. Go for the 1st class or any better than the standard class.

The most comfortable travel is with Regiojet private company. They have their own office separate from CD - České Dráhy (the main railway carrier of Czechia) at the Main train station. Or you can prebook your trip from Prague to Zábřeh na Moravě online. We recommend you book the Relax or Business class.

  • Step 1. Arrive at Prague Airport PRG

    Take the AE bus (The Airport Express bus) towards Prague MS (The main train station.) You'll need to pay cash in Czech Korunas directly to the driver.

  • Step 2. Prague Main Train station

    The AE (Airport Express) bus will leave you at the Main train station. Search for the 'CD - České Dráhy' or 'Regiojet' ticket office. Buy the train tickets towards Šumperk and don't forget to ask for the 1.st class or any class that offers wifi, airconditioning, snacks, and plenty space for kids.
    Once on the train, confirm with the person that checks the tickets, the estimated time for you to be able to to get off the train at Zábřeh na Moravě. They will be able to estimate the time for you to get ready to leave the train. This is very important as you need to switch the trains or take a taxi to Šumperk from there.

  • Step 3. Switch the trains or take a Taxi

    Once you get to Zábřeh na Moravě, get off the train and hop on the train to Šumperk or take a taxi that can bring you directly to your hotel.

Option 2: Brno Airport BRQ

Brno Airport > Main Train Station > Šumperk

There is a direct train connection from Brno Main train station to Šumperk, please confirm when buying the tickets.

Estimated travel duration: approx 3 hours

  • Arrive at Brno airport

    Take the bus towards the Brno Main train station. You can pay cash directly to the driver in CZK or EUR. Find more details

  • Arrive at Brno Train Station

    Get your ticket to Šumperk online or directly from the CD (Czech Railways) ticket office and make sure that you're buying a direct connection to Šumperk without any train change. Šumperk is the last stop and you don't need to worry about when to get off.

  • Arrive at Šumperk

    Get a taxi towards your hotel in front of the train station
